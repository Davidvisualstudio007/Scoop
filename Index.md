Mayo

Cut the bread down the middle, not all the way, leace a little connection on one of the sides connecting the top and bottom part of the bread.
lay down the bacon parallel to the bread, springkle a bit of mozarella, put it in the oven and press 6 inch double meat setting.
After the machine made a finished sound, take the food out of the machine and put in on the table.
put 2 cucumber on the bread side, spinach, onions, halapenos. drizzle a bit of sauces of choice and sprinkle salt and pepper.
Close the bread, wrap it up. tape the opening.
